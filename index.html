BSCL - Solution for retail investors

BSCL is a smart contract technology and distributed accounting technology developed for the purpose of splitting real estate under Tech - home.com, which utilizes blockchain’s intrinsic advantages such as trustless mechanism, anti- tamper, co-supervision and traceability.

Through BSCL’s smart contract, large real estate can be split and distributed to financial institutions. In this way, the user will get the opportunity to invest small amounts of money and benefit from real estate, and ensure security and low risk of investment."

BSCL uses its own smart contract and distributed accounting technology

BSCL uses its own smart contract and distributed accounting technology to connect the asset side and the financial institution side. Large assets can be split and distributed to multiple financial institutions through the BSCL smart contract.

Users, through financial institutions, can invest in small real estate split by BSCL smart contracts. BSCL, as the center of the connection of two ends, cannot be changed by any individual or organization after the asset is split and bound, including the BSCL itself.

Therefore, through BSCL blockchain ledger, it can be ensured that users’ investment will be firmly bonded to the pointed assets, so that investment on the platform is more secure and stable

BSCL has the following advantages:

- Make real estate investment downsizing dynamic and transparent

- High transaction convenience

- Information transparency

- BSCL can fragmentize relatively small property rights, and shorten the listing cycle

- BSCL makes housing digital assets easier and simpler
